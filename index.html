HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER

  1. Explore our curated collection and choose the products you like.
  2. Select your product, choose the correct size (if applicable), and add it to your bag.
  3. You can add multiple products from different categories in the same order.
  4. Choose colours/themes if the product has multiple options.
  5. Free shipping is available all over Kerala (orders accepted only from India).
  6. Go to your bag and review all selected items carefully.
  7. Click "Checkout" and read the order summary thoroughly.
  8. Enter your shipping address accurately in the provided fields.
  9. Double-check your address and order details to avoid mistakes.
  10. Click "Complete via WhatsApp" or "Complete via Instagram" to place your order.
  11. Wait for confirmation – our team will contact you shortly after receiving your order.
  12. For any queries, reach out via WhatsApp, Instagram, or Email using the icons on the homepage.
