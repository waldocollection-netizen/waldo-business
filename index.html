HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER

  1. Browse our curated collection and choose the products you like.
  2. Open the product page, select the correct size (if applicable), and add the item to your bag.
  3. You can add multiple products from different categories in a single order.
  4. If the product offers colour or theme options, select your preferred choice before adding it to the bag.
  5. We offer free shipping across Kerala. Orders are currently accepted only from within India.
  6. Once you finish selecting items, go to your bag to review all the products you have added.
  7. Click “Checkout” and carefully review the order summary.
  8. Fill in your shipping details accurately in the provided fields.
  9. Please double-check your address, phone number, and order details to avoid delivery issues.
  10. Click “Complete via WhatsApp” or “Complete via Instagram” to submit your order request.
  11. Your order details will be automatically prepared and sent through the selected platform.
  12. After receiving your order request, our team will review it and contact you shortly for confirmation.
